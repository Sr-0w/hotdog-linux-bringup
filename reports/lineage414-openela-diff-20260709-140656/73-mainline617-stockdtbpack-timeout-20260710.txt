## Interpretation

This rules out the simple hypothesis that the recent mainline hang is caused by
the entry12 simplefb/stdout-path modification in the DTB pack. The unmodified
stock full DTB pack produces the same no-USB/no-pstore/no-visible-output
timeout with the same pstore-enabled mainline kernel.

The next useful mainline work should move below initramfs and display-helper
instrumentation:

- kernel entry / `arch/arm64/kernel/head.S`
- early exception or panic path before pstore is available
- GIC/SRE/EL2 or PSCI behavior before normal device init

In parallel, the downstream 4.14 line remains the only validated visual and SSH
baseline. For screen-side progress, prioritize a downstream fbcon/simplefb or
early replay experiment rather than another unchanged mainline boot.
