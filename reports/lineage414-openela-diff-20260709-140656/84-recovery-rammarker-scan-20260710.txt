Recovery RAM-marker scan - 2026-07-10
======================================

Reason
------

The mainline RAM-marker candidate writes `ENT1`/`ENT2`/`SWT3` into the existing
`0xa9800000` ramoops dump window, but the recovery collector previously only
saved the raw dump. That meant a follow-up step had to remember the byte order
and search manually.

Collector change
----------------

`scripts/collect-recovery-crash-artifacts.sh` now writes:

```text
ramoops-marker-scan.txt
```

after the existing `ramoops-phys-a9800000-4m.img` capture attempt.

The scan reports:

```text
ENT1 little-endian bytes: 31 54 4e 45
ENT2 little-endian bytes: 32 54 4e 45
SWT3 little-endian bytes: 33 54 57 53
```

For each hit it prints both the file offset and physical address relative to
base `0xa9800000`. If the raw dump is absent or empty, it records
`missing-or-empty-dump` instead of failing the collection.

Scope
-----

This is a host-side reporting improvement only. It does not add any new device
write, flash, reboot, wipe, or partition modification.
