mainline617 RAM marker candidate - 2026-07-10
=============================================

Reason
------

The previous mainline 6.17 pstore-built test with the unmodified stock DTB pack
timed out before visible output, USB, pstore, or watchdog recovery. The next
useful mainline test needs evidence below initramfs. This candidate uses the
RAM marker `head.S` probe from report 79 while keeping the rest of the tested
`003000` boot shape as close as possible.

Suggested order

2. Use this `022522` candidate when the next objective is specifically to
   diagnose how far mainline reaches inside `head.S`.
3. Do not launch this while the phone is invisible; wait for stable pmOS SSH,
   recovery ADB, or fastboot first.
