87 - tty-kmsg screen candidate
================================

Date: 2026-07-10 03:25 CEST

Context
-------

The previous next image, 030600, already used the pmaports downstream 4.14
kernel package with VT/fbcon/simplefb enabled, the fixed entry12 simplefb DTB
pack, the framebuffer paint test, the initramfs DRM helper, and the rootfs
visible tty shell. Offline DTB-pack verification showed that entry 12 contains
the expected /chosen ranges, stdout-path, linux,stdout-path, display0 alias,
and simple-framebuffer node.

The remaining screen-side question is whether kernel/simplefb/fbcon text can
reach the panel before the DRM helper appears. The user reported that the
visible output comes after the bootloader logo, not as early kernel-verbose
text. That can mean fbcon does not bind to simplefb, that it binds but no
user-visible log dump is routed to the active tty early enough, or that the
initramfs never runs its normal setup_framebuffer path because splash is not on
the cmdline.

Builder change
--------------

scripts/build-watchdog-bootimg.sh now has:

  --tty-kmsg-console

When enabled, the initramfs gets hotdog_tty_kmsg_console.sh. The helper starts
after the rescue watchdog in both stage1 and stage2, before the DRM helper. It
waits for /dev/tty0, /dev/tty1, or /dev/console, waits up to 30 seconds for
/dev/fb0, sets an empty framebuffer mode from /sys/class/graphics/fb0/modes
when possible, boosts backlight, forces a high printk level, prints
cmdline/framebuffer state plus dmesg tail, and refreshes dmesg/status every
four seconds.
