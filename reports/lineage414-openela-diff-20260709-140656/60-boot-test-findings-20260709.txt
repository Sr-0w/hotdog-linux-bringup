  format x8r8g8b8, stdout-path display0.

- Candidate:

- Result: no immediate fastboot return, no adb, no USB network, no SSH, no
  telnet, and no USB reconnect observed by the host during the wait window.
- Interpretation: the bootloader accepted the full DTB pack and probably jumped
  into the kernel. The failure is now likely before initramfs watchdog/userspace
  or before USB gadget setup. This is a better signal than earlier immediate
  fastboot-return tests.
