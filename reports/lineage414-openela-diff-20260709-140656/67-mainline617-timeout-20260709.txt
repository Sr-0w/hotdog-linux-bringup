Implication
-----------
This is stronger than a normal userspace/SSH failure. The injected initramfs
watchdog did not produce a visible reboot after its 420s deadline, so the
mainline 6.17 path likely fails before the watchdog is started, or fails early
enough that the forced reboot path cannot execute.

The bootloader did not immediately reject the image back to fastboot, so the
next mainline investigation should focus on the earliest kernel path:

- kernel entry and Android boot image expectations
- DTB compatibility before initramfs
- early console / bootconsole visibility
- pstore/ramoops reachability after manual recovery
- differences between the downstream 4.14 Image that boots and this mainline
  Image before initramfs handoff
