extra config edit was made to staging during this pass.

Symbols not portable to staging
-------------------------------

The downstream 4.14 lineage config uses:

  CONFIG_FB_CMDLINE=y
  CONFIG_PSTORE_ZLIB_COMPRESS=y

Those symbols are not present in the local 6.8.7 or 6.17 kernel trees. The
modern pstore Kconfig uses CONFIG_PSTORE_COMPRESS, whose help text says it is
implemented with zlib deflate. Do not add dead CONFIG_PSTORE_ZLIB_COMPRESS or
CONFIG_FB_CMDLINE lines to staging unless a future tree actually defines them.
